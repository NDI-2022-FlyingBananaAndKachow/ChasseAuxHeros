SexInfo : Prevention through playo

As part of the "Nuit de l'info 2022" (Computer science night 2022) which notably marks the 34th World AIDS Day (every 1/12) we have made a game aimed at educating the younger generations in order to help them take charge of their sexuality in an uninhibited way and thus contribute to the fight against prejudice and STDs.

This project is brought to you by teams "Flying Banana" and "Kachow"
